About

Sridhar Verose has been San Ramon resident for over 12 years. He lives with his wife Sucharitha and two kids who attend San Ramon schools. He has been deeply involved in several community activities in the city. His four thousand plus volunteer service hours while living in San Ramon is testimony for his commitment to enhance quality of life within community.
 
He proved his strong leadership skills by working on Team building, negotiation and perseverance in execution of vision by creating a non-profit sports organization SRCA start from scratch. And today this organization is serving our San Ramon community along with other Tri-Valley communities in alignment with community goals like Health, Wellness, Social life and revenue generator for local small businesses. And his participation in year around community cultural events shows his appreciation for inclusion within community.
 
Sridhar graduated with MS Software Engineering from San Jose State University. He has been working in Information Technology sector for more than twenty four years. He is currently working as Senior Manager, Infrastructure Engineering at Opentext. His motivation for community services is passion to make world a better place to live.
